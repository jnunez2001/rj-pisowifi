One-Time Setup: Power Recovery
This device may not power itself back on automatically after an outage. Enter BIOS setup (usually by pressing Del or F2 right after powering on) and set "Restore on AC Power Loss" (also called "AC Power Recovery" or "After Power Failure") to "Power On." This is a one-time hardware setting. It can't be configured from software.
