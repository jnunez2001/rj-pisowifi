One-Time Setup: Power Recovery

This device may not turn itself back on automatically after a power outage. Enter your BIOS setup (usually by pressing Del or F2 right after powering on) and set "Restore on AC Power Loss" (sometimes called "AC Power Recovery" or "After Power Failure") to "Power On." This can't be set from software, it's a one-time step in the hardware itself.

How customers get internet access
A few things to know before turning this on:
  • Supports MikroTik routers on either RouterOS 6 or 7, including older or budget friendly models. More router brands may be supported in the future.
  • Your router's Hotspot service and DHCP server need to already be set up on the network you're connecting to. Bandwidth limits are applied per device once it has an IP address.
  • Customers go straight to your portal to pay and connect. They will never see the router's own login page.
  • This server still keeps track of session time and expiry. The router only reflects whether a device is currently allowed online.
